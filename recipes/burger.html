Brandon's Burger Recipe

Easy and great burger:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • Salt and pepper
  • 4 Hamburger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Pickles
  • Sliced cheese
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, or any condiment

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the burger patties
  2. Preheat the grill or pan
  3. Cook the patties
  4. Toast buns
  5. Assemble the burgers
