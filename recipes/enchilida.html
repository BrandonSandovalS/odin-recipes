Brandon's Enchilada Recipe

Easy and great enchiladas:

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breasts
  • Small onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Tea spoon of Cumin
  • Tea spoon chili powder
  • Tea spoon of vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Tortilla
  • 2 cups red enchilada sauce
  • 2 cups of shredded cheddar

Instructions:

  1. Cook chicken
  2. Prepare chicken filing
  3. Preheat oven
  4. Assemble enchiladas
  5. Roll and place in baking dish
  6. Pour sauce and cheese
  7. Bake
